Blood Bank and Donor Management System

1. Introduction

The Blood Bank and Donor Management System is a web-based platform designed to facilitate the management of blood donations, donor registrations, and blood bank inventories.

2. Objectives

  • Centralized system for managing blood donors and blood bank inventories.
  • Enable users to find and request blood in emergency situations.
  • Allow donors to register and schedule blood donations.
  • Facilitate communication between donors, recipients, and blood banks.
  • Maintain real-time records of available blood types and stock levels.
  • Automate donor eligibility checks and notifications.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Donors:

  • Register & Maintain Personal Health Profile
  • Check Eligibility for Blood Donation
  • Schedule Blood Donation Appointments
  • Receive Notifications for Next Eligible Donation Date
  • View Donation History & Certificates

Recipients / Patients:

  • Search for Available Blood by Blood Type & Location
  • Request Blood from Nearby Blood Banks or Donors
  • Track Request Status & Receive Alerts
  • Contact Blood Banks for Emergency Needs

Blood Bank Administrators:

  • Manage Blood Stock & Inventory Levels
  • Approve or Reject Blood Donation Requests
  • Verify Donor Eligibility Before Scheduling Donations
  • Track Blood Expiry & Generate Reports
  • Notify Donors About Blood Shortages & Urgent Requirements

Hospitals / Organizations:

  • Request Blood for Emergency Cases
  • Track Blood Supply & Demand Trends
  • Manage Hospital-Specific Donor Records

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Blood Donors
  • Patients / Recipients
  • Blood Banks
  • Hospitals & Clinics
  • NGOs & Health Organizations
  • Government Health Departments
