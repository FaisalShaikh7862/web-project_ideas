Online Parking Slot Booking System

1. Introduction

The Online Parking Slot Booking System is a web-based platform designed to allow users to find and book parking spaces in advance.

2. Objectives

  • Provide an easy-to-use platform for users to search and book parking slots online.
  • Help parking lot owners manage slot availability and bookings efficiently.
  • Enable cashless transactions for parking payments.
  • Reduce time spent searching for parking spaces, minimizing congestion.
  • Improve space utilization through real-time tracking and dynamic pricing (optional).

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Drivers / Users:

  • Register & Login to Personal Dashboard
  • Search for Available Parking Slots by Location & Time
  • Reserve Parking Slots in Advance
  • Make Secure Online Payments for Bookings
  • View Booking History & Receipts
  • Get Real-Time Slot Availability Updates
  • Receive Notifications for Booking Confirmation & Expiry

Parking Lot Owners / Managers:

  • Register & List Parking Locations with Slot Details
  • Manage Parking Availability & Bookings
  • Set Dynamic Pricing Based on Demand (Optional)
  • Accept & Process Online Payments
  • View Reports on Parking Utilization & Revenue
  • Generate Parking Passes & QR Codes for Entry

Administrators:

  • Manage User & Parking Lot Owner Registrations
  • Approve or Remove Parking Listings
  • Monitor Transactions & Resolve Disputes
  • Generate System Analytics & Reports
  • Ensure Compliance with Parking Regulations

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Drivers & Vehicle Owners
  • Parking Lot Owners & Operators
  • Shopping Malls & Commercial Complexes
  • Airports, Railways & Public Transport Stations
  • City Municipalities & Smart City Projects
