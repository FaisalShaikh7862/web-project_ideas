Court Case Management System

1. Introduction

The Court Case Management System is a web-based platform designed to digitize and streamline the management of court cases, legal proceedings, and judicial records. This system helps legal professionals, court clerks, and judges efficiently track case progress, schedule hearings, and maintain legal documents in a structured and secure manner.

2. Objectives

  • Provide a digital platform for managing court cases and legal records.
  • Improve efficiency in case tracking, scheduling, and documentation.
  • Enable secure document storage and retrieval for legal professionals.
  • Automate notifications and reminders for upcoming hearings.
  • Reduce manual paperwork and administrative workload in court proceedings.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Judges & Court Officials:

  • Register & Login to Secure Dashboard
  • View & Manage Assigned Cases
  • Schedule Hearings & Court Sessions
  • Review Legal Documents & Evidence Online
  • Update Case Status & Rulings

Lawyers & Attorneys:

  • Submit Case Details & Legal Documents
  • Track Case Progress & Upcoming Hearings
  • Upload & Access Court Orders & Judgments
  • Receive Notifications for Case Updates
  • Manage Client Profiles & Case History

Plaintiffs & Defendants (Public Users):

  • Register & Login to Check Case Status
  • Submit Legal Complaints & Petitions
  • View Case Proceedings & Court Orders
  • Receive Hearing Date Notifications
  • Upload Relevant Documents for Legal Review

Administrators (Court Clerks & System Admins):

  • Manage User Registrations & Permissions
  • Assign Cases to Judges & Legal Professionals
  • Maintain Court Schedules & Hearing Timetables
  • Generate Case Reports & Court Statistics
  • Ensure Data Security & Access Control

Additional Features:

  • Automated Case Numbering & Document Indexing
  • E-Signature Integration for Legal Approvals (Optional)
  • Digital Payment Gateway for Fine & Fee Payments
  • AI-Based Case Search & Legal Precedent Recommendations (Optional)
  • Multi-Court Management System for Different Jurisdictions

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx

Additional Technologies:

  • AJAX for Real-Time Case Updates
  • WebSockets for Instant Notifications
  • PDF Generator for Court Orders & Case Summaries
  • Secure Cloud Storage for Legal Documents
  • OCR (Optical Character Recognition) for Scanned Documents (Optional)

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Judges & Court Officials: To manage and track legal cases.
  • Lawyers & Attorneys: To file petitions, track hearings, and manage client cases.
  • Plaintiffs & Defendants (Public Users): To check case status and submit complaints.
  • Government & Judiciary Departments: To digitize legal records and improve case management efficiency.
