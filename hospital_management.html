Hospital Management System

1. Introduction

The Hospital Management System (HMS) is a web-based platform designed to streamline hospital operations by managing patient records, doctor appointments, billing, staff management, and inventory tracking. The system enables hospitals, clinics, and healthcare providers to improve efficiency, reduce paperwork, and provide better patient care.

2. Objectives

  • To digitalize hospital operations and reduce manual paperwork.
  • To manage patient appointments, medical records, and treatments efficiently.
  • To provide an online platform for booking doctor consultations.
  • To automate billing and payment processing.
  • To maintain real-time records of hospital inventory, staff, and departments.
  • To enhance hospital workflow management and improve patient care.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Patients:

  • Register & Login to Personal Dashboard
  • Book Appointments with Doctors (Online & Offline)
  • View Medical Records, Prescriptions, & Test Reports
  • Make Online Payments for Consultations & Treatments
  • Receive Notifications for Appointments & Health Checkups

Doctors:

  • Manage Patient Appointments & Schedules
  • Access Patient Medical History & Reports
  • Prescribe Medications & Upload Treatment Plans
  • Communicate with Patients via Online Consultation

Hospital Staff (Receptionists, Nurses, Pharmacists, etc.):

  • Manage Patient Registrations & Appointment Scheduling
  • Update Treatment Progress & Patient Health Records
  • Process Billing & Payments
  • Manage Medicine Stock & Hospital Inventory

Administrators (Hospital Management):

  • Manage Hospital Departments & Staff Profiles
  • Oversee Patient Admissions & Discharges
  • Generate Financial & Operational Reports
  • Monitor System Performance & Data Security

Additional Features:

  • Online Consultation & Telemedicine Integration
  • Emergency Patient Admission Management
  • Hospital Bed & Room Allocation System
  • Lab Test Booking & Diagnostic Reports Upload
  • Pharmacy & Inventory Management for Medicine Stock
  • AI-Based Chatbot for Basic Medical Queries (Optional)
  • Insurance Integration for Cashless Treatments

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx

Additional Technologies:

  • AJAX for Real-Time Appointment & Report Updates
  • jQuery for Interactive UI Elements
  • Payment Gateway API for Online Transactions
  • Google Maps API for Hospital Location & Navigation

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Hospitals & Clinics: Managing patient records, staff, and medical services.
  • Doctors & Medical Practitioners: Handling patient consultations and prescriptions.
  • Patients: Booking appointments, accessing reports, and managing health records.
  • Pharmacies: Managing medicine stock and prescriptions.
  • Laboratories & Diagnostic Centers: Handling test bookings and report generation.
  • Hospital Administrators: Overseeing operations, staff, and financial transactions.
