Bookstore Website

1. Introduction

The Bookstore Website is an online platform designed for book lovers to browse, purchase, and review books. It enables users to explore various genres, read descriptions and reviews, and securely purchase physical or digital copies.

2. Objectives

  • Create a seamless online bookstore experience.
  • Enable vendors to list and sell books efficiently.
  • Provide personalized book recommendations.
  • Offer secure payment options and order tracking.
  • Allow users to leave reviews and ratings.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Customers (Buyers):

  • Register/Login
  • Browse & Search Books
  • Add to Wishlist or Shopping Cart
  • Secure Checkout & Order Tracking
  • Read & Write Reviews

Book Vendors (Sellers/Publishers):

  • Register & Manage Seller Accounts
  • List Books with Descriptions & Prices
  • Manage Inventory & Orders
  • View Sales Reports & Customer Feedback

Administrators (Website Managers):

  • Manage Users & Vendors
  • Oversee Book Listings & Payments
  • Handle Promotions & Reports
  • Monitor Customer Queries & Support

Additional Features:

  • AI-Based Book Recommendations
  • E-Book & Audiobook Support
  • Live Chat Support
  • Discount Coupons & Loyalty Rewards
  • Book Rental & Subscription Plans

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (React.js or Vue.js)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Book Readers & Literature Enthusiasts
  • Authors & Publishers
  • Bookstore Owners
  • Libraries & Educational Institutions
