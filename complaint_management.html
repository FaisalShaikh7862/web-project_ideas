Complaint Management System

1. Introduction

The Complaint Management System is a web-based platform designed to streamline the process of lodging, tracking, and resolving complaints efficiently. It allows users to submit complaints, track their status, and receive updates, while administrators can categorize, prioritize, and resolve issues in an organized manner. This system helps organizations manage customer or employee grievances systematically, improving transparency and service quality.

2. Objectives

  • To provide an online platform for users to submit complaints easily.
  • To allow administrators to manage and resolve complaints efficiently.
  • To ensure real-time tracking and updates on complaint statuses.
  • To categorize complaints based on type and urgency.
  • To generate reports for insights into complaint resolution performance.
  • To enhance user satisfaction through quick grievance redressal.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Users (Complainants):

  • User Registration & Login
  • Submit Complaints with Details & Attachments
  • Track Complaint Status
  • Receive Notifications & Updates
  • Provide Feedback on Resolution

Administrators:

  • Dashboard to View & Manage Complaints
  • Categorize & Prioritize Complaints
  • Assign Complaints to Relevant Departments/Staff
  • Track Progress & Update Complaint Status
  • Generate Reports & Analytics

Staff / Department Handlers:

  • View Assigned Complaints
  • Update Progress & Resolution Status
  • Communicate with Complainants if Needed

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx
  • Additional Technologies:
    • AJAX for Real-Time Updates
    • jQuery for Interactive UI
    • Email/SMS Notification Integration

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Customers / General Users: Individuals who need to raise complaints.
  • Employees: Staff members who want to report workplace issues.
  • Organizations: Businesses, government agencies, or service providers managing grievances.
  • Administrators: System managers overseeing and ensuring complaint resolution.
