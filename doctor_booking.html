Doctor Appointment Booking System

1. Introduction

The Doctor Appointment Booking System is a web-based platform designed to allow patients to book appointments with doctors seamlessly.

2. Objectives

  • Digitize and simplify the doctor appointment booking process.
  • Allow patients to find doctors based on specialization, availability, and location.
  • Automate scheduling, cancellations, and reminders.
  • Enhance patient experience with real-time availability and instant confirmations.
  • Provide a secure and centralized system for managing patient-doctor interactions.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Patients:

  • Register/Login to Personal Dashboard
  • Search for Doctors by Specialization, Location & Availability
  • View Doctor Profiles, Experience, and Ratings
  • Book, Reschedule, or Cancel Appointments
  • Receive Appointment Confirmations & Reminders
  • Secure Online Payment for Consultation Fees (if applicable)

Doctors & Clinics:

  • Register & Create Doctor Profiles
  • Manage Appointments & Schedules
  • Approve or Decline Appointment Requests
  • Provide Virtual Consultations via Video Call

Administrators:

  • Manage Doctor & Patient Registrations
  • Oversee Appointment Scheduling & Conflicts
  • Generate Reports on Bookings & Cancellations

Additional Features:

  • Live Video Consultation for Remote Patients
  • AI-Powered Doctor Recommendations
  • E-Prescription Generation & Medical Report Uploads
  • Integration with Google Maps for Location Tracking

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (React.js or Vue.js)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Patients & General Public
  • Doctors & Clinics
  • Hospitals & Multi-Specialty Healthcare Centers
  • Telemedicine & Online Healthcare Startups
