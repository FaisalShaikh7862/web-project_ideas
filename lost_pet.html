Lost Pet Reporting and Adoption Portal

1. Introduction

The Lost Pet Reporting and Adoption Portal is a web-based platform designed to help pet owners report lost pets, find missing pets, and facilitate pet adoptions. The system connects pet owners, animal shelters, and adopters, ensuring a streamlined process for locating lost pets and finding new homes for rescued animals.

2. Objectives

  • To provide a centralized platform for reporting and finding lost pets.
  • To connect animal shelters, rescuers, and adopters.
  • To allow users to post adoption listings for pets in need of a home.
  • To facilitate secure communication between pet finders and owners.
  • To promote responsible pet adoption and rehoming.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Pet Owners:

  • Register & Login to Personal Dashboard
  • Report a Lost Pet with Details & Photos
  • Search Database for Found Pets by Location & Breed
  • Receive Alerts When a Matching Pet is Found
  • Contact Pet Finders Directly via Chat or Email

Pet Finders / Rescuers:

  • Report a Found Pet with Photos & Location
  • Browse Lost Pet Reports to Match with Found Pets
  • Contact Owners to Return Pets

Adopters:

  • View Available Pets for Adoption
  • Apply for Adoption & Contact Shelters / Owners
  • Receive Notifications for New Adoption Listings

Animal Shelters / NGOs:

  • List Pets Available for Adoption
  • Manage Adoption Requests & Approvals
  • Organize Adoption Events & Campaigns
  • Post Emergency Alerts for Rescued Pets

Administrators:

  • Manage User Accounts & Listings
  • Approve / Remove Suspicious Reports
  • Monitor Adoption Processes & System Analytics
  • Ensure Compliance with Animal Welfare Policies

Additional Features:

  • AI-Based Pet Matching (Optional) – Suggests Found Pets Similar to Lost Reports
  • Geo-Location Tracking – Helps Locate Lost Pets in Nearby Areas
  • Pet Care Tips & Adoption Guidelines Section
  • Social Media Integration for Wider Pet Search & Adoption Campaigns
  • Email & SMS Alerts for Lost & Found Pet Matches

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx

Additional Technologies:

  • Google Maps API for Location-Based Search
  • AJAX for Real-Time Pet Status Updates
  • jQuery for Interactive UI Elements
  • Cloud Storage for Pet Images & Documents

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Pet Owners: Individuals searching for their lost pets.
  • Animal Shelters & NGOs: Managing rescue and adoption processes.
  • Pet Finders & Rescuers: Reporting and returning found pets.
  • Adopters & Pet Lovers: Looking to adopt pets in need of a home.
  • Veterinary Clinics & Pet Welfare Organizations: Assisting with pet identification and medical records.
