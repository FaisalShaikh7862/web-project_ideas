Photography Portfolio and Booking System

1. Introduction

The Photography Portfolio and Booking System is a web-based platform designed for photographers to showcase their work, manage client bookings, and handle payments online.

2. Objectives

  • Provide photographers with an online platform to showcase their work.
  • Enable clients to view portfolios and book photography sessions easily.
  • Automate appointment scheduling and payment processing.
  • Facilitate communication between photographers and clients.
  • Provide a structured system for managing photography projects and bookings.

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx
