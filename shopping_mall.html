Smart Shopping Mall Management System

1. Introduction

The Smart Shopping Mall Management System is a web-based platform designed to enhance mall operations by digitizing store management, customer engagement, and facility administration. It helps mall administrators, store owners, and customers with real-time updates, automated billing, event promotions, and seamless navigation within the mall.

2. Objectives

  • Digitize and streamline mall operations for better management.
  • Provide a platform for store owners to manage their inventories, sales, and promotions.
  • Offer customers a smart shopping experience with real-time store updates and indoor navigation.
  • Automate rent management, utility billing, and complaint handling for mall administrators.
  • Promote events, discounts, and offers to boost foot traffic and sales.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Mall Administrators:

  • Register & Manage Stores, Store Owners, and Employees
  • Track Rent Payments, Utility Bills & Other Expenses
  • Handle Complaints & Maintenance Requests from Store Owners
  • Generate Reports on Sales, Customer Footfall & Revenue Trends
  • Manage Events, Advertisements & Promotional Campaigns

Store Owners:

  • Register & Manage Store Details (Products, Offers, Inventory)
  • Track Daily Sales & Revenue Reports
  • Manage Employee Attendance & Shift Scheduling
  • List Discounts & Special Offers on the Mallâ€™s Portal
  • Accept Online Orders for Pickup or Delivery (If Applicable)

Customers (Shoppers):

  • Browse Mall Stores, Available Products, and Offers
  • Use Indoor Navigation to Locate Stores & Services
  • Pre-Book Services (Movie Tickets, Salon, Restaurants, etc.)
  • Receive Personalized Promotions & Discounts
  • Submit Feedback & Complaints via the Mall App

Security & Maintenance Staff:

  • Manage & Monitor CCTV Feeds & Security Alerts
  • Handle Customer Complaints Related to Security Issues
  • Track Maintenance Requests & Resolve Issues Efficiently
  • Access Emergency Response System for Fire & Safety Incidents

Additional Features:

  • Smart Indoor Navigation with Real-Time Store Mapping
  • Automated Rent & Utility Billing System for Stores
  • AI-Powered Customer Behavior Analysis & Shopping Recommendations
  • QR-Based Entry & Exit System for Parking & Security Control
  • Loyalty & Rewards Program for Frequent Shoppers
  • Emergency Alert System for Security Threats or Fire Hazards
  • Digital Advertisement System to Promote Mall Events & Sales

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx
  • Additional Technologies:
    • Google Maps API for Indoor Navigation
    • WebSockets for Real-Time Updates & Notifications
    • AI for Customer Insights & Shopping Recommendations
    • IoT Integration for Smart Parking & Security Systems
    • Payment Gateway API for Online Payments & Billing

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Mall Administrators: To manage stores, rentals, security, and customer engagement.
  • Store Owners & Retailers: To handle inventory, sales, and promotions.
  • Shoppers & Customers: To explore stores, navigate the mall, and access discounts.
  • Security & Maintenance Staff: To monitor security and resolve maintenance issues.
