Employee Leave and Attendance Management System

1. Introduction

The Employee Leave and Attendance Management System is a web-based platform designed to automate and streamline employee attendance tracking and leave management.

2. Objectives

  • Centralized system for managing employee attendance and leave requests.
  • Enable employees to apply for leave and track attendance records.
  • Allow HR to approve/reject leave requests efficiently.
  • Automate payroll calculations (optional).
  • Generate detailed reports on employee attendance trends.
  • Improve transparency and efficiency in workforce management.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Employees:

  • Register & Log in to Personal Dashboard
  • Mark Daily Attendance
  • Apply for Leave with Supporting Documents
  • View Leave Balance & History
  • Check Work Schedule & Holidays

Managers / HR:

  • Approve or Reject Leave Requests
  • Monitor Employee Attendance
  • Generate Reports
  • Manage Work Shifts & Holiday Schedules

Administrators:

  • Manage Employee Profiles & Roles
  • Set Leave Policies & Holiday Calendars
  • Configure Notifications

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Small & Large Businesses
  • HR Departments
  • Government Offices & Institutions
  • Remote Work Teams
  • Administrators
