Job Portal Website

1. Introduction

The Job Portal Website is a web-based platform that connects job seekers with employers, providing a centralized system for job postings and applications.

2. Objectives

  • Provide a seamless platform for job seekers and employers to connect.
  • Allow employers to post job listings and manage applicants.
  • Enable job seekers to search, apply for jobs, and track applications.
  • Offer resume-building and career guidance tools.
  • Automate and simplify the hiring process.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Job Seekers:

  • Register/Login to Create a Profile
  • Upload Resume & Update Career Information
  • Search for Jobs Based on Category, Location, and Experience
  • Apply for Jobs & Track Application Status
  • Receive Job Alerts & Notifications

Employers:

  • Register/Login to Company Dashboard
  • Post Job Listings with Descriptions & Requirements
  • Manage Applications & Shortlist Candidates
  • Schedule Interviews & Communicate with Applicants

Administrators:

  • Manage Job Seeker & Employer Registrations
  • Approve or Remove Job Listings
  • Monitor Applications & Website Activity
  • Generate Reports on Hiring Analytics

Additional Features:

  • AI-Based Job Recommendations
  • Resume Parsing & Automated Profile Creation
  • Online Skill Assessment & Test Modules
  • Live Chat Support

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (React.js or Vue.js)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Job Seekers & Fresh Graduates
  • Employers & HR Recruiters
  • Staffing Agencies
  • Career Coaches & Resume Builders
