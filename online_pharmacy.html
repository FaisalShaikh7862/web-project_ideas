Online Pharmacy Store

1. Introduction

The Online Pharmacy Store is a web-based platform designed to enable users to purchase medicines and healthcare products online.

2. Objectives

  • Provide a convenient and user-friendly platform for ordering medicines online.
  • Allow users to upload prescriptions and get approved medications.
  • Enable pharmacies to manage inventory, sales, and customer orders efficiently.
  • Ensure secure payment processing and timely delivery of medicines.
  • Provide automated reminders for prescription refills and dosage tracking.
  • Improve healthcare accessibility through an efficient e-commerce system.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Customers / Users:

  • Register & Login to Personal Dashboard
  • Search for Medicines & Healthcare Products
  • Upload Prescriptions for Approval
  • Add Products to Cart & Place Orders
  • Make Secure Online Payments
  • Track Order Status & Delivery Updates
  • Receive Medicine Refill Reminders
  • Access Health Blogs & Medicine Information

Pharmacists / Store Managers:

  • Manage Medicine Inventory & Stock Levels
  • Verify & Approve Prescription Uploads
  • Process Orders & Track Deliveries
  • Generate Sales Reports & Analytics
  • Offer Discounts & Promotional Deals
  • Handle Customer Inquiries & Support

Administrators:

  • Manage User & Pharmacy Registrations
  • Approve or Remove Sellers & Products
  • Monitor Transactions & Sales Performance
  • Ensure Compliance with Health Regulations
  • Generate System Analytics & Reports

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Customers / Patients
  • Pharmacies & Medical Stores
  • Doctors & Healthcare Professionals
  • Hospitals & Clinics
  • Delivery Agents
