Vehicle Service Management System

1. Introduction

The Vehicle Service Management System is a web-based platform designed to streamline and manage vehicle maintenance and service operations efficiently. It allows customers to book service appointments online, track service progress, and receive notifications, while service centers can manage bookings, assign tasks to mechanics, and maintain service records. The system enhances customer experience, reduces manual paperwork, and ensures smooth operation of vehicle servicing businesses.

2. Objectives

  • To provide an online platform for booking vehicle service appointments.
  • To manage service center operations efficiently.
  • To enable customers to track the status of their vehicle servicing.
  • To facilitate seamless communication between customers and service centers.
  • To maintain digital records of service history for future reference.
  • To generate reports for service centers to analyze performance and revenue.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Customers:

  • User Registration & Login
  • Book Service Appointments
  • View & Track Service Status
  • Receive Notifications & Alerts
  • View Service History
  • Provide Ratings & Feedback

Service Centers / Admin:

  • Manage Service Bookings & Schedules
  • Assign Work to Mechanics
  • Track Vehicle Service Status
  • Maintain Customer & Vehicle Records
  • Generate Invoices & Billing
  • Manage Spare Parts Inventory
  • View Reports & Analytics

Mechanics:

  • View Assigned Service Tasks
  • Update Service Progress
  • Mark Services as Completed

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx
  • Additional Technologies: AJAX for real-time updates, jQuery for dynamic interactions, Email/SMS Notification Integration

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Customers – Vehicle owners who need maintenance or repair services.
  • Service Centers – Businesses that provide vehicle servicing and repairs.
  • Mechanics – Technicians responsible for performing vehicle repairs.
  • Administrators – System managers who oversee and manage service center operations.
