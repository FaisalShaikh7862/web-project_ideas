Gamers Market – In-Game Items & Accounts Selling & Purchasing Management System

1. Introduction

The Gamers Market System is an online platform designed for gamers to buy, sell, and trade in-game items, gaming accounts, and digital assets securely. It provides a marketplace where users can list their gaming assets, negotiate deals, and complete transactions safely. The system includes fraud prevention mechanisms and ensures secure payments to protect buyers and sellers.

2. Objectives

  • Create a marketplace for gamers to buy and sell in-game items and gaming accounts.
  • Ensure secure transactions and prevent fraudulent deals.
  • Offer a rating and review system for trustworthy transactions.
  • Provide a smooth user experience with easy listing, searching, and filtering of items.
  • Implement an escrow system for secure payments and transactions.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Buyers (Gamers Looking for Items/Accounts):

  • Register & Login to Personal Dashboard
  • Browse & Search In-Game Items or Accounts for Sale
  • Add Items to Wishlist & Compare Listings
  • Contact Sellers via Chat/Messaging System
  • Make Secure Payments via Escrow System
  • Receive Purchased Items/Accounts Securely

Sellers (Gamers Selling Items/Accounts):

  • Register & Verify Account for Secure Selling
  • List In-Game Items or Gaming Accounts for Sale
  • Set Pricing & Negotiate with Buyers
  • Accept Payments & Transfer Ownership of Items
  • Receive Ratings & Reviews from Buyers

Administrators:

  • Manage User Registrations & Verifications
  • Monitor Listings to Prevent Scams & Fraudulent Activities
  • Approve/Reject Item & Account Listings
  • Oversee Escrow Payment System & Dispute Resolution
  • Generate Reports on Sales, Transactions & User Activity

Additional Features:

  • Escrow Payment System to Prevent Fraud
  • Live Chat Support for Buyer-Seller Communication
  • Multi-Game Support (PUBG, CS:GO, Valorant, Fortnite, etc.)
  • Automated Delivery for Digital Items via API Integration
  • Referral & Loyalty Programs for Active Users
  • Seller Ranking System Based on Transaction History
  • AI-Based Price Suggestion for Market Fairness (Optional)

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx
  • Additional Technologies:
    • WebSockets for Live Chat & Instant Updates
    • AJAX for Real-Time Item Search & Filtering
    • Payment Gateway API (PayPal, Stripe, Crypto Payments)
    • Secure Authentication & Two-Factor Verification for Sellers
    • AI-Based Fraud Detection for Suspicious Transactions (Optional)

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Gamers: To buy & sell in-game items and accounts.
  • Game Traders: To manage bulk trades & transactions securely.
  • eSports Players: To purchase high-rank accounts & rare skins.
  • Game Developers: To integrate official in-game item selling features.
  • Online Gaming Communities: To facilitate a secure gaming marketplace.
