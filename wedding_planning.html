Wedding Planning and Management System - Synopsis

1. Introduction

The Wedding Planning and Management System is a web-based platform designed to streamline the planning and coordination of weddings. It serves as a one-stop solution for couples, wedding planners, vendors, and guests, enabling efficient organization of wedding-related tasks, budgeting, vendor selection, guest management, and event scheduling. This system helps reduce stress and ensures a smooth execution of wedding events.

2. Objectives

  • Provide an organized platform for managing wedding planning tasks.
  • Allow couples to create and track wedding budgets and expenses.
  • Facilitate vendor bookings for venues, catering, photography, etc.
  • Enable guest list management and RSVP tracking.
  • Offer event scheduling and task reminders.
  • Provide a seamless communication system between couples, planners, and vendors.

3. Functionality

User Roles & Features

Couples / Users:

  • Register & Create Wedding Profile
  • Plan & Manage Wedding Tasks with Checklists
  • Budget Planning & Expense Tracking
  • Manage Guest Lists & Send Invitations
  • Book Vendors for Venues, Catering, Photography, etc.
  • Create & Share Wedding Event Schedules
  • Receive Notifications & Reminders

Wedding Planners:

  • Create & Manage Multiple Wedding Projects
  • Assign Tasks & Track Progress
  • Manage Vendor Contacts & Bookings
  • Customize & Monitor Budgets
  • Generate Reports & Insights

Vendors (Caterers, Photographers, Decorators, etc.):

  • Register & Showcase Services
  • Receive & Manage Booking Requests
  • Communicate with Clients via Messaging System
  • Provide Service Quotes & Confirm Availability

Administrators:

  • Manage User Accounts & Vendor Listings
  • Approve or Remove Vendors & Services
  • Monitor Wedding Projects & System Analytics
  • Generate Reports on Wedding Trends & Vendor Performance

Additional Features:

  • Online Payment Integration for Vendor Booking (Optional)
  • Wedding Website Creation for Couples (Optional)
  • Customizable Wedding Theme & Color Coordination Tools
  • Integration with Social Media for Sharing Wedding Updates
  • AI-Based Vendor Recommendations Based on Budget & Preferences

4. Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap for UI)
  • Backend: PHP (Laravel or Core PHP)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Server: Apache or Nginx
  • Additional Technologies: AJAX, jQuery, Payment Gateway, Email & SMS Notifications

5. Who Can Use This System?

  • Couples & Families: Those planning weddings and managing events.
  • Wedding Planners & Event Coordinators: Professionals handling wedding management.
  • Vendors & Service Providers: Businesses offering wedding-related services.
  • Guests: Invited attendees viewing schedules and RSVPing to events.
  • Administrators: Platform managers overseeing wedding planning processes and vendor listings.
